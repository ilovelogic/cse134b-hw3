## A Step into the Past
This assignment is near and dear to my heart because I used to love cheese puffs (admittedely, the writer of this article would not deem the use of the word "cheese" in their name justified by their content, but hey, they bear a little resemblence at least).

I include here, for the reader's hoped for amusement, the ode I wrote to cheese puffs in my youth.

### An Ode to Annie's Cheese Puffs

Cheese puffs are so crunchy,
But they aren't as hard as rock.
And that is why, when I first tried them,
All I could do was gawk.

They were so delicious,
So perfect in every way.
When I eat cheese puffs,
They can really make my day.

Cheese puffs taste like Heaven,
Like something not found where we are,
So I asked my mom to get some,
And to drive us in her car.

Cheese puffs are so awesome,
Yes, they have the greatest taste,
And that is why to get them,
I like to make such haste.

Cheese puffs, you're so amazingly yummy,
Yes I wrote this just for you.
Yes, you really belong in my tummy.
Because you are delicious and new.
